ABOUT THE ASSOCIATION

H.S.A. Confluente is the study association of the Honors Academy at the Eindhoven University of Technology. H.S.A. Confluente aims a building a community where students from all of the different tracks come together to enjoy both educational and fun activities. When starting honors, students get to know the association through the awesome introduction weekend where they meet each other. From that point onwards, the community only grows further through different events like workshops from companies, game nights, dinners and an awesome study trip. Next to loads of fun and educational activities, students also get the opportunity to develop themselves professionally. This development is possible through network events and workshops for improving one's CV, or interview skills are provided for free. Additionally, H.S.A. Confluente is part of the Dutch Honors Community, which is a network of honors associations throughout the Netherlands. Together with these other associations, we regularly organize even more awesome activities.
