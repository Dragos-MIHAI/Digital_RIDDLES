Welcome to the digital riddles workshop!

Proceed to your first challenge

This event will be consisting of some online riddles created on a website platform. Teams of around 4 people each from different backgrounds (CS, ID, Physics...etc.) are going to be made. Furthermore each team will work together in solving the online game created by us. The game will have several levels and each of them will contain 3 riddles (each of them being based on a different topic art, math, physics, computer science). The principle will be something similar to an escape room. Once one riddles is passed, the team moves to the next level and the team who complete first all the levels win.
